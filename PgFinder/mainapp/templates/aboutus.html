About Us

Welcome to PG Kamra Bangalore – the easiest way to find your next PG room in Bangalore!

Created by a local developer (Dinesh, that’s me!), this platform was designed with one simple goal in mind: to make finding a room in Bangalore as smooth as possible. Whether you’re a student, a working professional, or just moving to the city, PG kamra Bangalore helps you discover rooms that fit your budget, preferences, and location.

Built using Django, the website is simple, fast, and reliable, helping you browse room listings, filter by your needs, and connect with landlords or roommates in very short time.

I created this project because I know how stressful it can be to find a good place in a city as big as Bangalore. So, I thought: why not make it easier?

Hope you find the perfect room – and if you’ve got any feedback or suggestions, I’d love to hear from you!
